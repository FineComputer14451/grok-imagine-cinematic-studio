---

## Step 1: Update Your Master Prompt

1. Download the new **`MASTER_PROMPT_v3.4.md`**
2. **Replace** your old `MASTER_PROMPT_v3.3.md` with the new v3.4 version

---

## Step 2: Update the Agent Files (Recommended)

For the best experience, update all 15 agent files to v3.4.
