## [3.1] - 2026-05-07

### Added
- **Quality Assurance Guardian v3.0** — New final gatekeeper agent with mandatory 10-point Full Bible Review and Go/No-Go decision system.
- **Mandatory QA Gate** in the master workflow — Studio Director now activates Quality Assurance Guardian before any generation.
- **Studio State Protocol v3.0** — Shared live `studio_state.json` across all agents for better coordination.
- **Self-Evaluation Layer** — Every agent output now includes structured 1–10 scoring across 5 key metrics.
- **Prompt Optimizer** in Imagine Prompt Master v3.0
- **Micro-Expression Library** in Performance & Emotion Director v3.0
- **Beat-Synced Audio Arc** in Sonic Architect v3.0
- **Momentum Vector** support in Cinematic Sequence Extender v3.0 and LAST_FRAME_RECAP
- **Three-Act Mini-Structure** + **Pacing Heatmap** in Narrative Arc & Pacing Strategist v3.0
- **Lens & Sensor Simulation** + precise Camera Move Curves in Director of Photography v3.0
- **Drift Detection System** in Continuity & Consistency Guardian v3.0
- **LUT Recommendation Engine** in Post-Production & Color Grading Supervisor v3.0
- **Prompt Complexity Scoring** in Workflow & Quota Optimizer v3.0
- **Kink-Specific Cinematic Template Library** + **Sensual Intensity Calibration** in ErosForge NSFW Director v3.0

### Changed
- Master prompt upgraded from v3.0 to **v3.1**
- All agents updated with consistent v3.0/v3.1 structure and Self-Evaluation blocks
- Improved collaboration protocols between agents
- Enhanced Master Extend from Frame Template with momentum vector

### Improved
- Overall consistency, emotional depth, and quota efficiency across the entire studio
- Final QA sign-off now required before generation
